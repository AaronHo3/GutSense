System Architecture

GutSense: Sensor to Screen

A toilet-mounted biosensor streams eight stool biomarkers to a FastAPI backend, where a two-stage neural risk model and a dual retrieval layer turn raw readings into a 0 to 100 risk score and AI-written clinical narratives, surfaced on patient and physician dashboards.
